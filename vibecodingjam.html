What is a VibeCodingJam?

A shared session for shipping something real.

A jam is a focused building session where people use AI tools to create, improve, or launch a small project. It can be casual, sponsored, local, online, beginner-friendly, or theme-based.

VibeCodingJam is the community engine of AI Vibing: a reason to gather, learn, finish, and show the work.
